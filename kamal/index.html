About Me

Kamal Sapkota is a passionate public administrator and community development professional serving the people of Galkot Municipality. With a strong commitment to transparency, equity, and sustainable progress, Kamal works tirelessly to bridge the gap between governance and the community.

From infrastructure planning to youth empowerment and social welfare, every initiative is driven by a people-first approach. Kamal believes in collaborative leadership and uses data-driven insights to craft policies that truly matter.
