Rotaract Club of Galkot Baglung

Strategic Blueprint & Plan of Action | Rota Year 2026-27

👁️ Our Vision

"To be a strong, community-driven Rotaract Club that empowers youth and brings sustainable development to Galkot."

⚡ Our Mission

"To foster leadership, innovation, and service through action-oriented projects that target education, healthcare, and professional readiness in our region."

🎯 Our Strategic Pillars

Membership Fellowship & Engagement

Focusing heavily on shifting from mere numbers to active youth engagement across Galkot. Ensuring every new member finds value, professional mentorship, and leadership pathways inside the club structure.

Community-First Initiatives

Developing long-term, high-impact regional initiatives in healthcare literacy, digital education integration in public schools, and climate resilience specialized for the hilly typography of Baglung District.

Digital Footprint & Rotary Synergy

Enhancing our grassroots communication channel networks, maintaining an active local press presence, and organizing deep joint fellowships with our parent Rotary Club of Baglung.

🚀 Club Goals (RY 2026-27)

85% Retention Rate

Retain existing members through customized leadership progression tracks and interactive professional development circles.

2 Signature Projects

Launch two long-term, sustainable signature projects within Galkot targeted at literacy and primary community healthcare infrastructure.

Rotary Citation Excellence

Fulfill all mandatory criteria laid down by Rotary International to achieve the Platinum level Rotary Citation.
