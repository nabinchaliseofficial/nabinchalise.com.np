✨ About Rotaract Club of Galkot Baglung

Embark on the dynamic journey of the Rotaract Club of Galkot Baglung, where visionary leaders have continuously inspired positive change. From our Chartered President, the visionary Rtr. Neetu Khadka, to the trailblazing leadership of Rtr. Anil Shrestha (President, RY 2020-21), the dynamic stewardship of Rtr. Ishor Gautam (President, RY 2022-23), the torch bearing dedication of Rtr. Kamal Kharel (President, RY 2023-24), the compassionate leadership of Rtr. Laxmi Pun (President, RY 2024-25), the progressive guidance of Rtr. Nabin Subedi (President, RY 2025-26), and the inspirational leadership of Rtr. Nabin Chalise (President, RY 2026-27), our club continues to thrive through the commitment and passion of its leaders.

Together, we are a diverse group of young leaders, professionals, and change-makers aged 16 years and above, passionately dedicated to creating meaningful impact by spreading kindness through service. Join us on this exciting journey of positive change and vibrant community engagement with the Rotaract Club of Galkot Baglung!

Established on April 23, 2020, under the leadership of our Chartered President, Rtr. Neetu Khadka, the Rotaract Club of Galkot Baglung is a vibrant team committed to placing service above self in a world often driven by material pursuits. As the heart and soul of the organization, we, as Rotaractors, regularly come together to discuss, plan, and implement projects that positively transform our community.

We envision ourselves as seeds of hope, leadership, and collaboration, committed to changing lives locally and globally, regardless of our numbers. Through fellowship, service, and professional development, we strive to inspire others and build a better world.
